The Shop

A chair, a mirror, and 15 years of steady hands.

Johnson's started as a single chair Mr. Johnson set up after 20 years cutting hair across the city. Today it's three barbers who take the craft seriously — and take their time. We learn your head, remember your cut, and get you back out the door looking sharp.

Every seat books online, every barber's got their own style, and there's always a fresh pot of coffee going.
